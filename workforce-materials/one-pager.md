# AI for Job Seekers — Workshop Overview

**A free 2-hour hands-on workshop for America's Job Center clients.**

---

## What It Is

Job seekers learn to use free AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude) for every stage of the job hunt — resume tailoring, cover letters, interview prep, skills gap analysis, and job search optimization. No technical background required. A smartphone is enough.

**Each participant leaves with:**
- A resume tailored to a real job they're targeting
- A cover letter written for that specific role
- 10 interview questions generated from the actual job description
- A skills gap report with free learning resources
- A repeatable weekly system for their job search

## What Participants Learn

1. **Resume Tailoring** — Feed a job description + resume to AI, get a targeted version in 60 seconds
2. **Cover Letters** — Generate a draft, then edit it until it sounds human
3. **Decoding Job Descriptions** — Extract unspoken requirements and ATS keywords
4. **Finding Hidden Jobs** — Discover job titles they'd never think to search for
5. **Interview Preparation** — Generate likely questions and practice with a mock interviewer
6. **Skills Gap Analysis** — Identify what's missing and get free resources to close the gap

---

## Who This Is For

- Job seekers at any skill level who have never used AI
- Career changers who need to translate their experience into a new field
- Long-term unemployed who need to refresh their approach
- Anyone who's been applying and not hearing back
- WIOA-eligible and general public — no restrictions

---

## Measured Outcomes

Every participant is surveyed at the workshop and followed up at 30 days. Tracked metrics:

- **Interview rate:** % who got at least one interview within 30 days
- **Placement rate:** % who received a job offer
- **Satisfaction:** Workshop rating (1–10)
- **Tool adoption:** % still using AI tools after 30 days

Data from initial pilots will be shared with the AJCC team to inform decisions about recurring sessions.
