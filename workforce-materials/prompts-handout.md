# AI Job Search Prompts — Take-Home Handout

Use these prompts with ChatGPT (chat.openai.com), Claude (claude.ai), or Gemini (gemini.google.com). All are free.

---

## 1. Resume Tailoring

```
I'm applying for this job:

[PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION HERE]

Here's my current resume:

[PASTE YOUR RESUME HERE]

Rewrite my resume to target this specific role. Keep it truthful — don't invent experience I don't have. Reorganize and rephrase my experience to highlight what this employer cares about. Use the same keywords from the job description. Format it cleanly, ready to copy-paste.
```

**After you get the result, clean it up:** Remove phrases like "I am excited to" or "passionate about." Add one specific number or result. Read it aloud — if it doesn't sound like you, change those parts.

---

## 2. Cover Letter

```
Write a cover letter for this job:

[PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION]

About me: [WRITE 2–3 SENTENCES ABOUT YOUR BACKGROUND IN YOUR OWN WORDS]

Rules:
- Keep it under 200 words
- No buzzwords (synergy, passionate, leverage, etc.)
- Open with something specific about THIS company
- Close with a clear next step

Write it like a thoughtful email, not a formal letter.
```

**Then ask:** "Make it shorter. Cut 30%." 

**Then ask:** "Does this cover letter sound like a human wrote it? Be honest."

---

## 3. Decode the Real Requirements

```
Here's a job description:

[PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION]

What are they ACTUALLY looking for that isn't explicitly stated? What would make someone stand out from other applicants? List the unspoken requirements.
```

---

## 4. Find Jobs You Didn't Know About

```
I'm a [YOUR CURRENT OR LAST JOB TITLE] with experience in [2–3 SKILLS]. What are 5 job titles I might qualify for that I wouldn't think to search for?
```

---

## 5. ATS Keyword Check (Critical!)

```
Extract the top 10 keywords from this job description that an ATS (applicant tracking system) would look for. List them by importance. Then tell me which ones are missing from my resume:

[PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION]
[PASTE YOUR RESUME]
```

---

## 6. Interview Question Generator

```
I'm interviewing for this role:

[PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION]

Based on this job description, what are the 10 most likely interview questions? Include:
- 3 technical/role-specific questions
- 3 behavioral questions (tell me about a time when...)
- 2 questions about my resume/background
- 2 curveball questions they might throw

For each question, explain what the interviewer is actually testing.
```

---

## 7. Mock Interview (Practice Out Loud!)

```
You're a hiring manager interviewing me for [JOB TITLE] at [COMPANY]. Ask me one question at a time. After I respond, give me brief feedback on my answer, then ask the next question. Start with "Tell me about yourself."
```

**For a harder version:** "Now interview me as a skeptical hiring manager who isn't sure I'm qualified. Challenge my answers."

---

## 8. Skills Gap Analysis

```
Here's my current resume:

[PASTE YOUR RESUME]

Here's the job I want:

[PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION]

What skills or experience am I missing? For each gap, tell me:
1. How big a deal is it (dealbreaker vs. nice-to-have)?
2. What free resource can I use to learn this in 2 weeks?
3. How do I address this gap in an interview without lying?
```

---

## 9. Salary Research & Negotiation

```
I'm applying for [JOB TITLE] in [CITY/REGION] with [X] years of experience. What's a realistic salary range? What factors could push me toward the higher end? Give me a script for negotiating if they offer the lower end.
```

## Your Weekly AI Job Search System

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| **Sunday night** | Find 5 jobs. Use Prompt #1 on each. Save 5 tailored resumes. |
| **Monday** | Apply to all 5. Use Prompt #2 for cover letters. Use Prompt #5 to check ATS keywords. |
| **Before each interview** | Use Prompts #6 and #7 for 15 minutes of prep. |
| **After each rejection** | Use Prompt #8. Ask "What was I probably missing?" |

---

## Quick Tips

- **You can use your phone for all of this.** No computer required.
- **The AI makes stuff up sometimes.** Always fact-check numbers and claims before using them.
- **AI writing sounds like AI writing.** Your job is to edit it until it sounds like you.
- **One good prompt beats ten mediocre ones.** Spend time on the job description and your input — better input = better output.
